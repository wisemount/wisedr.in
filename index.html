📋 How it works

A patient visit, step by step

See how a typical patient visit flows through WiseDR — from arrival to leaving with prescription in hand.

1
Patient arrives at reception
Receptionist searches by phone number — full history found instantly. New patient? Registered in under 2 minutes with UHID auto-assigned. Walk-in token issued, displayed on queue board.
2
Queue management
Doctor sees the live queue on their screen. Calls patient #7. Receptionist sees the status update immediately. If internet drops — the queue board still works fully offline on the device.
3
Consultation & prescription
Doctor records vitals, writes prescription with medicine search, notes the Ayurveda or Siddha assessment if applicable. Allergy check runs automatically. Saves in seconds.
4
Billing and payment
Receptionist creates the GST invoice with one click. Patient pays by UPI, card or cash. Receipt SMS sent automatically. Invoice marked as paid, balance updated in real time.
5
Patient receives everything
Prescription PDF lands on patient's portal. Receipt SMS sent. If a follow-up was set, a reminder SMS goes on that date automatically. Patient can view everything from their phone, anytime.
